About Me

The teaching faculty is remarkable, versatile and committed. They are friends, guides and motivating spirits, inspiring hearts, who strive to foster right values and knowledge. Special emphasis is given to the professional growth of staff members which keeps them tuned with the latest trends and innovations in the various fields of education. Refresher courses, seminars, workshops are all part of our efforts to make our faculty more than just educators

The Teaching Methodology

The teaching methodology aims at the holistic growth of the students. The school aims to create an ethos that will encourage children to grow physically in knowledge and most importantly in curiosity, confidence, independence, resilience and resourcefulness. It provides an environment where the mind can grow unfettered and free of constraints.

Evolution

A system of comprehensive and continuous evaluation based on oral and written examination is practiced throughout the year. This has proved to be a very effective and accurate method of evaluation. In the Secondary and Senior Secondary classes, evaluation is done as per the C.B.S.E. rules
